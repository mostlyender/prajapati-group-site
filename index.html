The Foundation

Amrat Prajapati, CPA, founded the firm as a single tax practice in one small office, on a simple promise: get the numbers right, every time. That reputation, earned one client at a time, became the foundation everything since has been built upon.

Cross-Border Scale

Prashant A. Prajapati, CPA, grew the practice into an international firm, serving companies and families both at home and across borders, and building the deep cross-border acumen that remains a defining strength of the group today.

Every Layer, Powered

Today, Prashant Prajapati, CPA, and Rohan Prajapati, CPA, build side by side, extending that same rigor beyond tax and accounting into controllership and strategic finance leadership, tech & AI, capital deployment, and talent, with a dedicated company for every layer of how a modern business runs.

II · The Idea

Most firms own one box on the org chart. We built a company for every one.

A business does not run on a single function. It runs on the tax position and the books, on the systems and the people, and on the capital behind all of it. So for each layer of the modern finance function we built a dedicated company, each one deliberate and excellent in its own right, and designed to work as one. The result is rare: a single group that can carry a company from the daily close to long-term capital, without ever leaving our ecosystem.

Breadth is only an advantage if every piece is built to the same standard.

Depth, not sprawl

We do not add companies to look bigger. We build each one deliberately, backed by strong domestic and cross-border resources, the same way we built Prajapati & Company more than three decades ago. Depth in each discipline is the point, and the integration is what sets us apart.

Built to flex

We work with companies of every size, from those just finding their footing to enterprises approaching a billion dollars in revenue, staying flexible and adaptable, and meeting each business exactly where it is.
